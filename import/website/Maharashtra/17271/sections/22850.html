Maximum period for which an undertrial prisoner can be detained.<\/b>-- Where a person has, during\r\nthe period of investigation, inquiry or trial under this Code of an offence under any law (not being an offence for\r\nwhich the punishment of death has been specified as one of the punishments under that law) undergone detention for\r\na period extending up to one-half of the maximum period of imprisonment specified for that offence under that law,\r\nhe shall be released by the Court on his personal bond with or without sureties:<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>Provided further that no such person shall in any case be detained during the period of investigation, inquiry or\r\ntrial for more than the maximum period of imprisonment provided for the said offence under that law.<\/br>
