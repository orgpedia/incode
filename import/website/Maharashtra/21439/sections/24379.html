Recovery of money due from an employer.--<\/b>(1) Where any money is due to a workman\r\nfrom an employer under a settlement or an award or under the provisions of 3<\/sup>[Chapter VA or Chapter\r\nVB], the workman himself or any other person authorised by him in writing in this behalf, or, in the case\r\nof the death of the workman, his assignee or heirs may, without prejudice to any other mode of recovery,\r\nmake an application to the appropriate Government for the recovery of the money due to him, and if the\r\nappropriate Government is satisfied that any money is so due, it shall issue a certificate for that amount to\r\nthe Collector who shall proceed to recover the same in the same manner as an arrear of land revenue:<\/br>
