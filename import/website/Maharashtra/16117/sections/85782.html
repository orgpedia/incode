\r\n<\/span>(3<\/i>) The Arbitrator shall then give a notice in the prescribed manner to the person interested in the land the possession of which is to be taken by Arbitrator requiring him\r\nto give possession of his land to the Arbitrator or any person authorised by him in this behalf within a period of one month from the date of service of notice ; and if no possession is delivered within the period specified in the notice, the Arbitrator shall take possession of the land and shall handover the land to the Planning Authority.
