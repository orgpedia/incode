\r\n<\/span>(3) The term for which the Court directs the offender to be imprisoned in default of payment of a fine\r\nshall not exceed one-fourth of the term of imprisonment which is the maximum fixed for the offence, if\r\nthe offence be punishable with imprisonment as well as fine.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(4) The imprisonment which the Court imposes in default of payment of a fine or in default of\r\ncommunity service may be of any description to which the offender might have been sentenced for the\r\noffence.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(5) If the offence is punishable with fine or community service, the imprisonment which the Court\r\nimposes in default of payment of the fine or in default of community service shall be simple, and the term\r\nfor which the Court directs the offender to be imprisoned, in default of payment of fine or in default of\r\ncommunity service, shall not exceed,---<\/br>

\r\n<\/span><\/span>(b) four months when the amount of the fine does not exceed ten thousand rupees; and<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(c) one year in any other case.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(6) (a) The imprisonment which is imposed in default of payment of a fine shall terminate whenever\r\nthat fine is either paid or levied by process of law;<\/br>
