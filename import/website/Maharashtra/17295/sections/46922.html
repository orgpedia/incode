\r\n<\/span>Insertion of new sections 12A,12B and 12C in Act XXI of 1860.--<\/b> After section 12 of the Societies\r\nRegistration Act, 1860 (XXI of 1860), the following shall be inserted, namely:--<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>\"12A. Change of name.--<\/b>Any society registered under this Act may, with the consent of not less\r\nthan two-thirds of the total number of its members' by a resolution at a general meeting convened for\r\nthe purpose and subject to the provisions of section 12B, change its name.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>12B. Notice of change of name.--<\/b>(1) Notice in writing of every change of name, signed by the\r\nSecretary and, by seven members of the society changing its name, shall be sent to the Registrar;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(2) If the proposed name is identical with that by which any other existing society has been\r\nregistered or, in the opinion of the Registrar, so nearly resembles such name as to be likely to deceive\r\nthe public, the Registrar shall refuse to register the change of name.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(3) Save as provided in sub-section (2), the Registrar shall, if he is satisfied that the provisions of\r\nthis Act in respect of change of name have been complied with, register the change of name and the\r\nchange of name shall have effect from the date of such registration.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>12C. Effects of change of name.--<\/b>The change in the name of a society registered under this Act\r\nshall not affect any rights or obligations of the society or render defective any legal proceeding by or\r\nagainst the society, and any legal proceeding which might have been continued or commenced by or\r\nagainst it by its former name may be continued or commenced by or against it by its new name\".<\/br>
