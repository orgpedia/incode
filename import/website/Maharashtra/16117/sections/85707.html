1<\/sup>[CHAPTER III-A<\/center><\/br>
\r\n
AREA DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY<\/center><\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(1<\/i>) The State Government may, for the purpose of securing planned\r\ndevelopment of areas within the State, declare by notification in the Official Gazette<\/i>, any area in the State to be a development area.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(2<\/i>) Every notification issued under sub-section (1<\/i>) shall define the limits of the area to which it relates.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3<\/i>) The State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette<\/i>, amalgamate two or more development areas into one development area, sub-divide any development area into different development areas and include such sub-divided areas in any other development area.\r\n\r\n<\/br>"}
