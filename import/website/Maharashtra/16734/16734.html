Act ID: 199726
Act Number: 26
Enactment Date: 1997-04-28
Act Year: 1997
Short Title: The Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997
Long Title: An Act to make special provisions for mobilisation of resources for completion of .some ongoing irrigation projects in time bound manner, for promotion and operation of the said irrigation projects in Vidarbha region and command area development and scheme for generation of hydro electric energy to harness the water of Godavari and Tapi Rivers allocated to the State of Maharashtra under the Water Disputes Tribunal Award and other allied and incidental activities including flood control by establishing the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Water Resources Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
