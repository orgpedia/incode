\r\n<\/span>(3) If the acts alleged constitute an offence falling within two or more separate definitions of any law\r\nin force for the time being by which offences are defined or punished, the person accused of them may be\r\ncharged with, and tried at one trial for, each of such offences.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(4) If several acts, of which one or more than one would by itself or themselves constitute an offence,\r\nconstitute when combined a different offence, the person accused of them may be charged with, and tried\r\nat one trial for the offence constituted by such acts when combined, and for any offence constituted by any\r\none, or more, of such acts.<\/br>
