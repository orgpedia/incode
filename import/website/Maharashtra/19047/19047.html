Act ID: 195555
Act Number: 55
Enactment Date: 1955-12-22
Act Year: 1955
Short Title: The Maharashtra Highways Act
Long Title: An Act to provide for the restriction of ribbon development along highways, for the prevention and removal of encroachment thereon, for the construction, maintenance and development of highways, for the levy of betterment charges and for certain other matters.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Public Works Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
