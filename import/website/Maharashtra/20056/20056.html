SCHEDULE B to the Bombay General Clauses Act, 1886 (Bom. Act III of 1886) Verbal Amendments made in the Regulations and Acts of the Governor of Bombay in Council
Show all section The Maharashtra General Clauses Act. PRELIMINARY GENERAL DEFINITIONS GENERAL RULES OF CONSTRUCTION POWERS AND FUNCTIONARIES PROVISIONS AS TO ORDERS, RULES, ETC., MADE UNDER ENACTMENTS MISCELLANEOUS
Section 1. Short title.

Section 1A. Extension of application of Act to Acts, rules, etc., made on or after the 1st day of November, 1956.

Section 1B. Extension of application of Act to Acts, rules, etc., of State of Maharashtra.

Section 2. Repeal.

Section 3. Definitions.

Section 4. Application of certain foregoing definitions to previous Bombay Acts.

Section 5. Coming into operation of Bombay Acts or Maharashtra Acts.

Section 6. Printing of date on which Act is published after having received the assent of the Governor, Governor-General, or His Majesty as the case may require.

Section 7. Effect of repeal.

Section 7A. Repeal of any enactment making textual amendment in any Act.

Section 8. Revival of repealed enactments.

Section 9. Construction of references to repealed enactments.

Section 10. Commencement and termination of time.

Section 11. Computation of time.

Section 12. Measurement of distance.

Section 13. Gender and number.

Section 14. Powers conferred on any Government to be exercisable from time to time.

Section 15. Power to appoint to include power to appoint ex-officio.

Section 16. Power to appoint to include power to suspend or dismiss.

Section 17. Substitution of functionaries.

Section 18. Successors.

Section 19. Official chiefs and subordinates.

Section 20. Construction of orders, etc., issued under Bombay Acts or Maharashtra Acts.

Section 21. Power to make to include power to add to, amend, vary or rescind, orders, etc.

Section 22. Making of rules or by-laws and issuing of orders between publication and commencement of Bombay Act or Maharashtra Act.

Section 23. Publication of orders and notifications in the Official Gazette to be deemed to be due publication.

Section 24. Provisions applicable to making of rules or by-laws after previous publication.

Section 25. Continuation of orders, etc., issued under enactments repealed and re-enacted.

Section 26. Recovery of fines.

Section 27. Provision as to offences punishable under two or more enactments.

Section 28. Meaning of service by post.

Section 29. Citation of enactments.

Section 30. Saving for previous Act, rules and by-laws.

Section 31. Application of Act to Ordinances and Regulations.
