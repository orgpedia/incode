\r\n<\/span>\r\n(2) The Grievance Redressal Committee shall consist of equal number of members from the\r\nemployer and the workmen.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(3) The chairperson of the Grievance Redressal Committee shall be selected from the employer and\r\nfrom among the workmen alternatively on rotation basis every year.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(4) The total number of members of the Grievance Redressal Committee shall not exceed more than\r\nsix:<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\nProvided that there shall be, as far as practicable, one woman member if the Grievance Redressal\r\nCommittee has two members and in case the number of members are more than two, the number of women\r\nmembers may be increased proportionately.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(5) Notwithstanding anything contained in this section, the setting up of Grievance Redressal\r\nCommittee shall not affect the right of the workman to raise industrial dispute on the same matter under\r\nthe provisions of this Act.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(6) The Grievance Redressal Committee may complete its proceedings within thirty days on receipt\r\nof a written application by or on behalf of the aggrieved party.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(7) The workman who is aggrieved of the decision of the Grievance Redressal Committee may\r\nprefer an appeal to the employer against the decision of Grievance Redressal Committee and the\r\nemployer shall, within one month from the date of receipt of such appeal, dispose off the same and send\r\na copy of his decision to the workman concerned.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(8) Nothing contained in this section shall apply to the workmen for whom there is an established\r\nGrievance Redressal Mechanism in the establishment.]<\/br>
