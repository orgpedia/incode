Act ID: 198158
Act Number: 58
Enactment Date: 1981-09-25
Act Year: 1981
Short Title: The Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulation of Employment and Welfare) Act, 1981
Long Title: An Act for regulating the employment of Private Security Guards employed in factories and establishments in the State of Maharashtra and for making better provisions for their terms and conditions of employment and welfare through the establishment of a Board therefor, and for matters connected therewith.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Industries, Energy and Labour Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
