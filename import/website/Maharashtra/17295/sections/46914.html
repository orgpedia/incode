\r\n<\/span>Insertion of new section 4A in Act XXI of 1860.--<\/b> After section 4 of the principal Act, the following\r\nnew section shall be inserted, namely:--<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\"4A. \r\n\r\n\r\n<\/span>Orissa<\/b><\/br>
\r\n\r\n<\/span>Insertion of new sections 4-A, 4-B and 4-C, Act (21 of 1860).--<\/b>In the Societies Registration Act,\r\n1860 (21 of 1860) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) after section 4, the following new sections\r\nshall be inserted, namely:--<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>4-A Changes in the list mention 4 and rules to be filed.--<\/b>(1) Without prejudice to the provisions\r\nof section 4 any change in personnel on the list filed under the said section occurring during the year to\r\nwhich such list relates shall be intimated to the Registrar or Societies within two months of such change.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(2) A copy of every alteration made in the rules and regulations of the Society, certified to be a\r\ncorrect copy by not less than three of the Governors, Directors or Members of the governing body, as the\r\ncase may be, shall be sent to the Registrar or Societies within two months of the making of such\r\nalteration.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>4-B. Persons by whom lists, etc, are to be sent.--<\/b>It shall be the duty--<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(a) Of the Chairman or, as the case may be, the President, the Secretary or any other person\r\nauthorized in that behalf by the rules and regulations of the Society or by a resolution of the governing\r\nbody of the Society; or<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(b) Of the Chairman or, as the case may be, the President of the governing body of the Society where\r\nis no such authorization, to file the list mentioned in section 4, or to send the intimation or as the case may\r\nbe, the copy mentioned in section 4-A to the Registrar of Societies.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>4-C. Offence.--<\/b>(1) If any person who is required so do to under the preceding section fails without\r\nreasonable cause to comply with the provisions thereof, he shall, on conviction, be punishable with fine\r\nwhich may extend to one hundred rupees.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(2) If any person willfully makes or causes to be made any false entry or alternation in, or any\r\nomission from, the list filed under section 4 or any statement or copy of rules and regulations sent to the\r\nRegistrar of Societies under section 4-A, he shall, on conviction, be punishable with fine which may\r\nextend to five hundred rupees.\"<\/br>
