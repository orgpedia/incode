\r\n<\/span>(2) When any person offending under sub-section (1) is under sentence of imprisonment for life, he\r\nmay, if hurt is caused, be punished with death or with imprisonment for life, which shall mean the\r\nremainder of that person's natural life.<\/br>

Illustrations.<\/center><\/i><\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(a) A shoots at Z with intention to kill him, under such circumstances that, if death ensued, A would\r\nbe guilty of murder. A is liable to punishment under this section.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(b) A, with the intention of causing the death of a child of tender years, exposes it in a desert place. A\r\nhas committed the offence defined by this section, though the death of the child does not ensue.<\/br>
