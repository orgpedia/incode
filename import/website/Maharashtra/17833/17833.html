Short Title: The Street Vendors (Protection of Livelihood and Regulation of Street Vending) Act, 2014
Long Title: An Act to protect the rights of urban street vendors and to regulate street vending activities and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Urban Development Department
Enforcement Date: 03-06-2014
Type: CENTRAL ACT (Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, )

Notification: 3rd June, 2014, vide notification No. S.O. 655(E), dated 4th March, 2014, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec. 3(ii).
