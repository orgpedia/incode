\r\n<\/span><\/span>(a) if the offence is punishable with death, be punished with imprisonment of either description\r\nfor a term which may extend to five years, and shall also be liable to fine;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(b) if the offence is punishable with imprisonment for life, or with imprisonment which may\r\nextend to ten years, be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend\r\nto three years, and shall also be liable to fine;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(c) if the offence is punishable with imprisonment which may extend to one year, and not to ten\r\nyears, be punished with imprisonment of the description provided for the offence for a term which\r\nmay extend to one-fourth part of the longest term of imprisonment provided for the offence, or with\r\nfine, or with both.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>Explanation.<\/i>\u2014 \"Offence\" in this section includes any act committed at any place out of India, which,\r\nif committed in India, would be punishable under any of the following sections, namely, 103, 105, 307,\r\nsub-sections (2), (3) and (4) of section 309, sub-sections (2), (3), (4) and (5) of section 310, 311, 312,\r\nclauses (f) and (g) of section 326, sub-sections (4), (6), (7) and (8) of section 331, clauses (a) and (b) of\r\nsection 332 and every such act shall, for the purposes of this section, be deemed to be punishable as if the\r\naccused person had been guilty of it in India.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>Exception.<\/i>\u2014This section shall not extend to any case in which the harbour or concealment is by the\r\nspouse of the offender.<\/br>
\r\n
Illustration.<\/i><\/center><\/br>
\r\n<\/span>A, knowing that B has committed dacoity, knowingly conceals B in order to screen him from legal\r\npunishment. Here, as B is liable to imprisonment for life, A is liable to imprisonment of either description\r\nfor a term not exceeding three years, and is also liable to fine.<\/br>
