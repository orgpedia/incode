Act ID: 20146
Act Number: 6
Enactment Date: 2014-03-20
Act Year: 2014
Short Title: The Maharashtra National Law University Act, 2014.
Long Title: An Act to establish and incorporate National Law Universities in the State for the development and advancement of legal education and for the purposes of imparting specialized and systematic instruction, training and research in systems of law and for the matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Higher and Technical Education Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
