Act ID: 20013
Act Number: 3
Enactment Date: 2001-01-02
Act Year: 2001
Short Title: The MAHARASHTRA WATER CONSERVATION CORPORATION ACT, 2000.
Long Title: An Act to make special provisions for promotion, operation and rapid development and regulation of watershed and water conservation works, including social forestry and irrigation projects in the State of Maharashtra by establishing the Water Conservation Corporation.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Soil And Water Conservation Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
