Act Number: 41
Enactment Date: 1965-09-13
Act Year: 1965
Short Title: The Maharashtra Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Boards Act, 1965
Long Title: An Act to provide for the establishment of a State Board and Divisional Boards to regulate certain matters pertaining to secondary (and higher secondary) education in the State of Maharashtra.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: School Education and Sports Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
