\r\n<\/span><\/span>(i)\"organised crime syndicate\" means a group of two or more persons who, acting either singly\r\nor jointly, as a syndicate or gang indulge in any continuing unlawful activity;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(ii) \"continuing unlawful activity\" means an activity prohibited by law which is a cognizable\r\noffence punishable with imprisonment of three years or more, undertaken by any person, either singly\r\nor jointly, as a member of an organised crime syndicate or on behalf of such syndicate in respect of\r\nwhich more than one charge-sheets have been filed before a competent Court within the preceding\r\nperiod of ten years and that Court has taken cognizance of such offence, and includes economic\r\noffence;<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>(2) Whoever commits organised crime shall,---<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span><\/span>(a) if such offence has resulted in the death of any person, be punished with death or\r\nimprisonment for life, and shall also be liable to fine which shall not be less than ten lakh rupees;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span><\/span>(b) in any other case, be punished with imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than five\r\nyears but which may extend to imprisonment for life, and shall also be liable to fine which shall not\r\nbe less than five lakh rupees.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3) Whoever abets, attempts, conspires or knowingly facilitates the commission of an organised\r\ncrime, or otherwise engages in any act preparatory to an organised crime, shall be punished with\r\nimprisonment for a term which shall not be less than five years but which may extend to imprisonment\r\nfor life, and shall also be liable to fine which shall not be less than five lakh rupees.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(4) Any person who is a member of an organised crime syndicate shall be punished with\r\nimprisonment for a term which shall not be less than five years but which may extend to imprisonment\r\nfor life, and shall also be liable to fine which shall not be less than five lakh rupees.<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>Provided that this sub-section shall not apply to any case in which the harbour or concealment is by\r\nthe spouse of the offender.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(6) Whoever possesses any property derived or obtained from the commission of an organised crime\r\nor proceeds of any organised crime or which has been acquired through the organised crime, shall be\r\npunishable with imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than three years but which may extend to\r\nimprisonment for life and shall also be liable to fine which shall not be less than two lakh rupees.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(7) If any person on behalf of a member of an organised crime syndicate is, or at any time has been in\r\npossession of movable or immovable property which he cannot satisfactorily account for, shall be\r\npunishable with imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than three years but which may extend to\r\nimprisonment for ten years and shall also be liable to fine which shall not be less than one lakh rupees.\r\n<\/br>
