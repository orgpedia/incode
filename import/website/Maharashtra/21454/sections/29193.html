Compounding of offences.--<\/b>(1) Any offence punishable under sections 47, 48 and 49 may,\r\neither before or after the institution of the prosecution, on an application by the alleged offender, be\r\ncompounded by payment of compounding amount not more than fifty thousand by such officer or\r\nauthority as the appropriate Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify in this\r\nbehalf:<\/br>
