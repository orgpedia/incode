\r\n<\/span><\/span>(b) may direct payment to the vendor or lessor of all the rents and profits which have accrued in\r\nrespect of the property from the date on which possession was so obtained by the purchaser or lessee\r\nuntil restoration of possession to the vendor or lessor, and, if the justice of the case so requires, the refund of any sum paid by the vendee or the lessee as earnest money or deposit in connection with the\r\ncontract.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3) If the purchase or lessee pays the purchase money or other sum which he is ordered to pay under\r\nthe decree within the period referred to in sub-section (1), the court may, on application made in the same\r\nsuit, award the purchaser or lessee such further relief as he may be entitled to, including in appropriate\r\ncases all or any of the following reliefs, namely:--<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>(4) No separate suit in respect of any relief which may be claimed under this section shall lie at the\r\ninstance of a vendor, purchaser, lessor or lessee, as the case may be.<\/br>
