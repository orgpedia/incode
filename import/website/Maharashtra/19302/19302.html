Act Number: 14
Enactment Date: 2013-04-22

Short Title: The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013
Long Title: An Act to provide protection against sexual harassment of women at workplace and for the prevention and redressal of complaints of sexual harassment and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Women and Child Development Department
Enforcement Date: 09-12-2013
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
Notification: 9th December, 2013, vide notification No. S.O. 3606(E), dated 9th December, 2013, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec. 3(ii).
