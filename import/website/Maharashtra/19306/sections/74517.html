\r\n<\/span>(3<\/i>) The Government shall communicate to the Board its views with reference to the results of the inspection or inquiry and may, after ascertaining the opinion of the Board thereon, advise it on the action to be taken, and fix a time limit for taking such action.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(4<\/i>) The Board shall report to the Government such action, if any, as it has taken or proposes to take upon the results of the inspection or inquiry. Such report shall be submitted with the opinion of the Board thereon, within such time as the Government may direct.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(5<\/i>) Where the Board does not within the time fixed, takes action to the satisfaction of the Government, the Government may, after considering any explanation furnished or representation made by the Board, issue such directions as it may think fit, and the Board shall comply with such directions. <\/br>"}
