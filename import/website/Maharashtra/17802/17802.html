Act ID: 20064
Act Number: 4
Enactment Date: 2006-01-20
Act Year: 2006
Short Title: The Commissions for Protection of Child Rights Act, 2005
Long Title: An Act to provide for the constitution of a National Commission and State Commissions for Protection of Child Rights and Children's Courts for providing speedy trial of offences against children or of violation of child rights and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Women and Child Development Department
Enforcement Date: 05-02-2007
Type: CENTRAL ACT (Ministry of Women and Child Development, )
Location: Maharashtra
Notification: 5th February, 2007, vide notification No. S.O. 229(E), dated 15th February, 2007, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec.3(ii).
