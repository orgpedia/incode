Act ID: 201323
Act Number: 23
Enactment Date: 2013-08-21
Act Year: 2013
Short Title: The Maharashtra State Board of Nursing and Paramedical Education Act, 2013.
Long Title: An Act to provide for the establishment of a State Board to regulate matters pertaining to the Diploma Level Nursing and Paramedical Education in the State of Maharashtra and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Medical Education and Drugs Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
