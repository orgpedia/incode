Act Number: 33
Enactment Date: 2006-08-11
Act Year: 2006
Short Title: The Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) Act, 2005.
Long Title: An Act to provide for a comprehensive law to abolish the practice of dedication of women as Devdasis to Hindu deities, idols, objects of worship, temples or religious institutions, and to protect the women so dedicated against exploitation, and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Women and Child Development Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
