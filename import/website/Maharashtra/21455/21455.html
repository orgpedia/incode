Act Number: 36
Enactment Date: 2020-09-28
Act Year: 2020
Short Title: The Code on Social Security, 2020
Long Title: An Act to amend and consolidate the laws relating to social security with the goal to extend social security to all employees and workers either in the organised or unorganised or any other sectors and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Industries, Energy and Labour Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
Ref Act ID: 202036
