Short Title: The prohibition of employment as manual scavengers and their rehabilitation act, 2013
Long Title: An Act to provide for the prohibition of employment as manual scavengers, rehabilitation of manual scavengers and their families, and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Social Justice and Special Assistance Department
Enforcement Date: 06-12-2013
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
Notification: 6th December, 2013, vide notification No. S.O. 2989(E), dated 1st October, 2013, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, Sec. 3(ii).
