Act ID: 196846
Act Number: 46
Enactment Date: 1968-09-02
Act Year: 1968
Short Title: The Insecticides Act, 1968
Long Title: An Act to regulate the import, manufacture, sale, transport, distribution and use of insecticides with a view to prevent risk to human beings or animals, and for matters connected therewith.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Dairy Development and Fisheries Department
Enforcement Date: 01-03-1971
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
Notification: 1st March, 1971, vide notification No. G.S.R. 300, dated 27th February, 1971, in respect of ss. 4, 7, 8 and 36, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec. 3(i). 1st August, 1971, vide notification No. G.S.R. 1108, dated the 28th July, 1971, in respect of remaining provisions see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec. 3(i).
