\r\n<\/span>(2<\/i>) He shall be entitled to be present, with the right to speak, at any meeting of any other\r\nauthority or body of the University but, shall not be entitled to vote thereat, unless he is the\r\nChairperson or member of that authority or body.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3<\/i>) The Vice-Chancellor shall have the power to convene meetings of any of the authorities,\r\nbodies or committees, as and when he considers it necessary so to do.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(@I@4@/I@) The Vice-Chancellor shall ensure that directions issued by the Chancellor are strictly\r\ncomplied with or, as the case may be, implemented.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(5<\/i>) It shall be the duty of the Vice-Chancellor to ensure that the provisions of the Act, Statutes,\r\nOrdinances, Rules and Regulations are strictly observed and that the decisions of the authorities, bodies\r\nand committees which are not inconsistent with the Act, Statutes, Ordinances, Rules or Regulations are\r\nproperly implemented.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(6<\/i>) The Vice-Chancellor may defer implementation of a decision taken or a resolution passed by\r\nany authority, body or committee of the University if, he is of the opinion that the same is not \r\nconsistent with the provisions of this Act, Statues, Ordinances, Rules or Regulations or that such\r\ndecision or resolution is not in the interest of the University and at the earliest opportunity refer it back\r\nto the authority, body or committee concerned for reconsideration in its next meeting with reasons to\r\nbe recorded in writing. If differences persist, he shall within a week, giving reasons submit it to the\r\nChancellor for decision and inform about having done so to the members of the authority, body or\r\ncommittee concerned. After receipt of the decision of the Chancellor, the Vice-Chancellor shall take\r\nthe action as directed by the Chancellor and inform the authority, body or committee concerned\r\naccordingly.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(7<\/i>) If there are reasonable grounds for the Vice-Chancellor to believe that there is an emergency\r\nwhich requires immediate action to be taken, he shall take such action, as he thinks necessary, and shall\r\nat the earliest opportunity, report in writing, the grounds for his belief that there was an emergency, and\r\nthe action taken by him, to such authority or body as would, in the ordinary course, have dealt with the\r\nmatter. In the event of a difference arising between the Vice-Chancellor and the authority or body\r\nwhether there was in fact an emergency, or on the action taken (where such action does not affect any\r\nperson in the service of the University), or on both, the matter shall be referred to the Chancellor whose\r\ndecision shall be final :<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>Provided that, where any such action taken by the Vice-Chancellor affects any person in the\r\nservice of the University, such person shall be entitled to prefer, within thirty days from the date of the\r\nreceipt of the notice of such action, an appeal to the Management Council.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(8<\/i>) Where any matter is required to be regulated by Statutes, Ordinances, Rules or Regulations\r\nbut no Statutes, Ordinances, Rules or Regulations are made in that behalf, the Vice-Chancellor may,\r\nfor the time being, regulate matter by issuing such directions as the Vice-Chancellor thinks necessary,\r\nand shall at the earliest opportunity thereafter, place them before the Management Council or other\r\nauthority or body concerned for approval. \r\n<\/span>(9<\/i>) The Vice-Chancellor shall be the appointing authority for the University teachers on the\r\nrecommendations of the selection committee constituted for the said purpose and after approval by the\r\nManagement Council.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(10<\/i>) The Vice-Chancellor shall be the appointing authority for officers of the University of the\r\nrank of Assistant Registrar and of the rank equivalent thereto and above and after approval by the\r\nManagement Council.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(11<\/i>) As the Chairperson of the authorities or bodies or committees of the University, the Vice-Chancellor shall be empowered to suspend a member from the meeting of the authority, body or\r\ncommittee for persisting to obstruct or stall the proceedings or for indulging in behaviour unbecoming\r\nof a member, and shall report the matter accordingly, to the Chancellor.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(12<\/i>) The Vice-Chancellor shall place before the Management Council a report of the work of the\r\nUniversity periodically as provided under the Ordinances.<\/br>
