\r\n<\/span>Provided that the Magistrate may order the father of a minor female child referred to in clause (b) to make such\r\nallowance, until she attains her majority, if the Magistrate is satisfied that the husband of such minor female child, if\r\nmarried, is not possessed of sufficient means:<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>Explanation.<\/i>--For the purposes of this Chapter,<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>(3) If any person so ordered fails without sufficient cause to comply with the order, any such Magistrate may,\r\nfor every breach of the order, issue a warrant for levying the amount due in the manner provided for levying fines,\r\nand may sentence such person, for the whole or any part of each months 4<\/sup>[allowance for the maintenance or the\r\ninterim maintenance and expenses of proceeding, as the case may be,] remaining unpaid after the execution of the\r\nwarrant, to imprisonment for a term which may extend to one month or until payment if sooner made:<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>Provided that no warrant shall be issued for the recovery of any amount due under this section unless\r\napplication be made to the Court to levy such amount within a period of one year from the date on which it became\r\ndue:<\/br>
