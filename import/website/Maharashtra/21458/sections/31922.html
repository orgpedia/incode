\r\n<\/span>\r\n\"61A. Confiscation by Forest officer of forest produce where forest offence is believed to have\r\nbeen committed.--<\/b>(1) Notwithstanding anything contained in the foregoing provisions of this Chapter or\r\nany other law, where a forest offence is believed to have been committed in respect of timber,\r\nsandalwood, firewood, charcoal or any other notified forest produce which is the property of State\r\nGovernment, the officer seizing the property under sub-section (1) of section 52 shall, without any\r\nunreasonable delay produce it, together with all tools, boats, vehicles and cattle used in committing such\r\noffence, before an officer authorised by the State Government in this behalf by notification in the Official\r\nGazette, not being an officer below the rank of an Assistant Conservator of Forests (hereinafter referred to\r\nas \"the authorised officer\").<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(2) The State Government may authorize one or more officers for any local area under sub-section\r\n(1).<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3) Where an authorised officer seizes under sub-section (1) of section 52 any timber, sandalwood,\r\nfirewood, charcoal or any other notified forest produce which is the property of the State Government or\r\nany such property is produced before an authorised officer under sub-section (1) and he is satisfied that a\r\nforest offence has been committed in respect of such property, such authorised officer may, whether or\r\nnot a prosecution is instituted for the commission of such forest offence, order confiscation of the\r\nproperty so seized together with all tools, boats, vehicles and cattle used in committing such offence.<\/br>
\r\n(4) (a) Where the authorised officer, after passing an order of confiscation under sub-section (3), is\r\nof the opinion that it is expedient in the public interest so to do, he may order the confiscated property or\r\nany part thereof and the tools, boats, vehicles and cattle to be sold by public auction,<\/br>
\r\n\r\n(b) Where any confiscated property or the tools, boats, vehicles and cattle are sold, aforesaid, the\r\nproceeds thereof, after deduction of the expenses of any such auction or other incidental expenses relating\r\nthereto shall, where the order of confiscation made under this section is set aside or annulled by an order\r\nunder section 61C or 61D, be paid to the owner thereof or to the person from whom it was seized as may\r\nbe specified in such order.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n61B. Issue of show cause notice before confiscation under section 61A.--<\/b>(1) No order confiscating\r\nany timber, sandalwood, firewood, charcoal or any other notified forest produce, tools, boats, vehicles or\r\ncattle shall be made under section 61A except after notice in writing to the person from whom it is seized\r\nand considering his objections, if any :<\/br>
\r\nProvided that no order confiscating a motor vehicle shall be made except after giving notice in\r\nwriting to the registered owner thereof, if in the opinion of the authorised officer it is practicable to do so,\r\nand considering his objections, if any.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(2) Without prejudice to the provisions of sub-section (1), no order confiscating a tool, boat, vehicle\r\nor cattle shall be made under section 61A if the owner of the tool, boat, vehicle or cattle proves to the\r\nsatisfaction of the authorised officer that it was used in carrying the timber, sandalwood, firewood,\r\ncharcoal or any other notified forest produce without the knowledge or connivance of the owner himself,\r\nhis agent, if any, and the person in charge of the tool, boat, vehicle or cattle and that each of them had\r\ntaken all reasonable and necessary precautions against such use.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n61C. Revision.--<\/b>Any Forest Officer not below the rank of Conservator of Forests specially\r\nempowered by the State Government in this behalf by notification in the Official Gazette, may suo motu<\/i>\r\ncall for and examine the record of the order of the authorised officer under section 61 A; and may make\r\nsuch inquiry or cause such inquiry to be made and may pass such orders as he deems fit :<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>\r\n61D. \r\n<\/span>(2) An order of the Session Judge under sub-section (1) shall be final and shall not be questioned in\r\nany Court.<\/br>
