Act Number: 30
Enactment Date: 1979-06-11

Short Title: The Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1979
Long Title: An Act to regulate the employment of inter-State migrant workmen and to provide for their conditions of service and for matters connected therewith.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Industries, Energy and Labour Department
Enforcement Date: 02-10-1980
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
Notification: 2nd October, 1980, vide notification No. G.S.R. 513(E), dated 11th August, 1980, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec. 3(i).
