\r\n<\/span>Explanation <\/i>2.---When a person causes one thing to resemble another thing, and the resemblance\r\nis such that a person might be deceived thereby, it shall be presumed, until the contrary is proved,\r\nthat the person so causing the one thing to resemble the other thing intended by means of that\r\nresemblance to practise deception or knew it to be likely that deception would thereby be practised;<\/br>

\r\n<\/span><\/span>(e) A writing containing directions or instructions is a document.<\/br>
