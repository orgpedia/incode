Act Number: 67
Enactment Date: 1957-12-28
Act Year: 1957
Short Title: The Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957
Long Title: An Act to provide for the development and regulation of mines and minerals under the control of the Union.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Industries, Energy and Labour Department
Enforcement Date: 01-06-1958
Type: CENTRAL ACT (Ministry of Mines, )
Location: Maharashtra
Notification: 1st June, 1958, vide notification No. G.S.R. 432, dated 29th May, 1958, see Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, sec. 3(i).
