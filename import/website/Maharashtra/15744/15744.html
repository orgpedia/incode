Act ID: 19984
Act Number: 4
Enactment Date: 1998-01-06
Act Year: 1998
Short Title: The Maharashtra Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997
Long Title: An Act to make special provisions for promotion and operation of irrigation projects, command area development and schemes for generation of hydroelectric energy to harness the water of Tapi River pertaining to the State of Maharashtra and other allied and incidental activities including flood control in the Tapi River Valley by establishing the Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Water Resources Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
