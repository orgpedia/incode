\r\n<\/span>(2) As from the commencement of this Code, each of the Presidency-towns of Bombay, Calcutta and Madras and the city of Ahmedabad shall be deemed to be declared under sub-section (1) to be a metropolitan area.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3) The State Government may, by notification, extend, reduce or alter the limits of a metropolitan area but the reduction or alteration shall not be so made as to reduce the population of such area to less than one million.<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>(5) Where the State Government reduces or alters, under sub-section (3), the limits of any\r\nmetropolitan area, such reduction or alteration shall not affect any inquiry, trial or appeal pending immediately before such reduction or alteration before any Court or Magistrate, and every such inquiry, trial or appeal shall continue to be dealt with under this Code as if such reduction or alteration had not taken place.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>Explanation.<\/i>--In this section, the expression \"population\" means the population as ascertained at the last preceding census of which the relevant figures have been published.<\/br>
