\r\n<\/span>(2) If the liability of the insurers to the employee is less than the liability of the employer to the\r\nemployee, the burden of proof shall lie on the employee for the balance in the insolvency proceedings or\r\nliquidation.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(3) Where in any case such as is referred to in sub-section (1), the contract of the employer with the\r\ninsurers is void or voidable by reason of non-compliance on the part of the employer with any terms or\r\nconditions of the contract (other than a stipulation for the payment of premium), the provisions of that subsection shall apply as if the contract were not void or voidable, and the insurers shall be entitled to prove in\r\nthe insolvency proceedings or liquidation for the amount paid to the employee:<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>Provided that the provisions of this sub-section shall not apply in any case in which the employee fails\r\nto give notice to the insurers of the happening of the accident and of any resulting disablement as soon as\r\npracticable after he becomes aware of the institution of the insolvency or liquidation proceedings.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(4) There shall be deemed to be included among the debts which under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy\r\nCode, 2016 (31 of 2016) or under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (18 of 2013) are in the\r\ndistribution of the assets of an insolvent or in the distribution of the assets of a company being wound up\r\nto be paid in priority to all other debts, the amount due in respect of any compensation, the liability accrued\r\nbefore the date of the order of adjudication of the insolvent or the date of the commencement of the winding\r\nup, as the case may be, and the provisions of that Code and Act shall have effect accordingly.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(5) Where the compensation is a half-monthly payment, the amount due in respect thereof shall, for the\r\npurposes of this section, be taken to be the amount of the lump sum for which the half-monthly payment\r\ncould, if redeemable, be redeemed if applications were made for that purpose under section 80, and a\r\ncertificate of the competent authority as to the amount of such sum shall be conclusive proof thereof.<\/br>
