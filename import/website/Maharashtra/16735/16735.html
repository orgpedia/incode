Act Number: 29
Enactment Date: 1979-09-12
Act Year: 1979
Short Title: The Maharashtra Tax on Buildings (with larger Residential Premises) (Re-enacted) Act, 1979.
Long Title: An Act to provide for levy of tax on buildings in Corporation areas in the State of Maharashtra, which contain larger residential premises.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Urban Development Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
