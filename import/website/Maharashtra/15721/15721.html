Act Number: 48
Enactment Date: 1976-11-11
Act Year: 1976
Short Title: The Maharashtra Jeevan Authority Act, 1976.
Long Title: An Act to provide for establishment of a (Jeevan Authority) for rapid development and proper regulation of water supply and sewerage services in the State of Maharashtra.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Water Supply and Sanitation Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
