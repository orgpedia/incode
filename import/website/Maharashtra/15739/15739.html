Act ID: 199823
Act Number: 23
Enactment Date: 1998-08-17
Act Year: 1998
Short Title: The Maharashtra Godawari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1998
Long Title: An Act to make special provisions for promotion and operation of irrigation projects, command area development and schemes for generation of hydro-electric energy to harness the water of Godawari River pertaining to the State of Maharashtra and other allied and incidental activities including flood control in the Godavari River Valley by establishing the Godawari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Water Resources Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
