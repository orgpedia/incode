\r\n<\/span><\/span>\r\n(a) effective enclosure of the plant or machinery used in the process;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>\r\n(b) removal or prevention of the accumulation of such dust, gas, fume or vapour;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>\r\n(c) exclusion or effective enclosure of all possible sources of ignition.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(2) Where in any factory the plant or machinery used in a process such as is referred to in\r\nsub-section (1)<\/i> is not so constructed as to withstand the probable pressure which such an explosion as\r\naforesaid would produce, all practicable measures shall be taken to restrict the spread and effects of the\r\nexplosion by the provision in the plant or machinery of chokes, baffles, vents or other effective\r\nappliances.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\n(3) Where any part of the plant or machinery in a factory contains any explosive or inflammable gas\r\nor vapour under pressure greater than atmospheric pressure, that part shall not be opened except in\r\naccordance with the following provisions, namely:--<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>\r\n(a) before the fastening of any joint of any pipe connected with the part or the fastening of the\r\ncover of any opening into the part is loosened, any flow of the gas or vapour into the part of any such\r\npipe shall be effectively stopped by a stop-valve or other means;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>\r\n(b) before any such fastening as aforesaid is removed, all practicable measures shall be\r\ntaken to reduce the pressure of the gas or vapour in the part or pipe to atmospheric pressure;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>\r\n(c) where any such fastening as aforesaid has been loosened or removed effective measures shall\r\nbe taken or prevent any explosive or inflammable gas or vapour from entering the part or pipe until\r\nthe fastening has been secured, or the case may be, securely replaced;<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>\r\nProvided that the provisions of this sub-section shall not apply in the case of plant or machinery\r\ninstalled in the open air.<\/br>
