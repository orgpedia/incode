Act ID: 201921
Act Number: 21
Enactment Date: 2019-07-31
Act Year: 2019
Short Title: The Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act, 2019
Long Title: An Act to provide for a comprehensive mechanism to ban the unregulated deposit schemes, other than deposits taken in the ordinary course of business, and to protect the interest of depositors and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Finance Department
Enforcement Date: 2019-2-21
Type: CENTRAL ACT (Ministry of Finance, Department of Financial Services)
Location: Maharashtra
Ref Act ID: A2019-21
