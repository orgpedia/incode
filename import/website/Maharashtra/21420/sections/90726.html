\r\n<\/span><\/span>(a) A instigates B to give false evidence. B, in consequence of the instigation, commits that offence.\r\nA is guilty of abetting that offence, and is liable to the same punishment as B.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span><\/span>(b) A and B conspire to poison Z. A, in pursuance of the conspiracy, procures the poison and delivers\r\n<\/span>it to B in order that he may administer it to Z. A is guilty of abetting that\r\noffence by conspiracy, and is liable to the punishment for murder.<\/br>
