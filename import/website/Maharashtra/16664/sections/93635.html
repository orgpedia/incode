\r\n\r\n<\/span>(4<\/i>) The Vice-Chancellor shall ensure that directions issued by the Chancellor are strictly\r\ncomplied with or, as the case may be, implemented.<\/br>

\r\n\r\n<\/span>Explanation.-<\/i> For purposes of this sub-section, action taken by the Vice-Chancellor shall not\r\ninclude disciplinary action taken against any employee of the university.<\/br>
