Short Title: The Maharashtra Employment Guarantee Act, 1977.
Long Title: An Act to make effective provision for securing the right to work by guaranteeing employment to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work in rural areas in the State of Maharashtra.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Planning Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
