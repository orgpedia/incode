Short Title: The Maharashtra Public Universities Act, 2016
Long Title: An Act to provide for academic autonomy and excellence, adequate representation through democratic process, transformation, strengthening and regulating higher education and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
Ministry: Ministry of Govt of Maharashtra
Department: Higher and Technical Education Department
Type: STATE
Location: Maharashtra
