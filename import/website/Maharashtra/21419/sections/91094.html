\r\n<\/span>(3) Where the notice issued to any person under sub-section (2) specifies any property as being held by\r\nany other person on behalf of such person, a copy of the notice shall also be served upon such other person.<\/br>

\r\n<\/span>Provided that if such person does not appear before the Court or the Magistrate or represent his case\r\nbefore the Court or Magistrate within a period of fourteen days specified in the show-cause notice, the\r\nCourt or the Magistrate may proceed to pass the ex parte order.<\/br>
\r\n<\/span>(5) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (2), if the Court or the Magistrate is of the\r\nopinion that issuance of notice under the said sub-section would defeat the object of attachment or seizure,\r\nthe Court or Magistrate may by an interim order passed ex parte direct attachment or seizure of such\r\nproperty, and such order shall remain in force till an order under sub-section (6) is passed.<\/br>
